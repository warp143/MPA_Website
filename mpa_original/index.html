Word From The President

MPA President, Dr. Daniele Gambero.

Welcome to you, the visitor of the Malaysia PropTech Association website. Let me give you a very short introduction of what our association is and what is offering to all our members, associates, visitors, and more. 

What we do is simple, we have been pulling together as many as possible of the Malaysian tech start-ups in property and construction with the aim of becoming a loud voice towards the implementation of digital transformation in the built environment.

  • Making Malaysia Proptech Association The Recognised Industry Association in Malaysia and beyond

  • Exposing all our members to international VCs and funding opportunities

  • Making MPA a global reference association of emerging markets

  • Seeing at least one of the Malaysian Proptech/ConTech startups, member of MPA, to raise to unicorn status

Read More

People

People shall be the main focus of our association and all our members. When addressing pain points and problem statements from the property industry stakeholders people should always be the main beneficiary of our members’ digital solutions. We respect and are committed in helping the growth of everyone notwithstanding disabilities, sex, race or religion and there shall never be a compromise on this. In our association woman will always be given the same opportunities as man and the association is strongly committed towards the achievement of Goal: 5 of SDG-2030 “Gender Equality”

Why Join MPA?

Strong Outreach Through Events

Participate in our local and internationally promoted events.

MPA believes in a strong outreach within the local tech world, but also into the local property and construction industry. Events both virtual and physical provide the ultimate opportunity to obtain knowledge and meet like-minded people.

Partnerships Form Our Foundation

Respecting each other’s values drives innovation.

We fully understand that taking on one of the largest industries in the country and feeding them with the latest technology and innovations, is a mammoth task. Only through strategic partnerships, we will succeed to reach even the furthest outcrops of the industry.

Funding Moves Businesses To Greater Heights

In a relatively young industry start-ups and scale-ups flourish and thrive.

Asia’s strong funding ecosystem is gaining even more strength with international players taking a peek at the Asian PropTech landscape continuously.

Funding for the industry, both local and overseas are important drivers for technology and homegrown innovations.
