Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about MPA

How do I become a member of MPA?

To become a member, visit our membership page and choose the tier that best fits your needs. Fill out the application form and our membership committee will review it within 5-7 business days.

What are the benefits of MPA membership?

MPA membership provides access to events, networking opportunities, industry insights, mentorship programs, and a platform to showcase your innovations to the Malaysian PropTech ecosystem.

How can I participate in MPA events?

Members get priority registration for events, while non-members can also attend most events by registering through our events page. Some events may have member-only access.

Can I partner with MPA for events or initiatives?

Yes! MPA actively seeks partnerships with organizations that share our vision. Contact our partnership team through the contact form or email us directly.
