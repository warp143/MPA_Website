ABOUT MPA

The Malaysia PropTech Association (MPA) is the premier organization driving innovation in Malaysia's property technology sector. Founded in 2019, we connect startups, established companies, and professionals to accelerate the digital transformation of Malaysia's real estate industry.

OUR MISSION

To foster a vibrant PropTech ecosystem that promotes innovation, sustainability, and inclusive growth across Malaysia's real estate sector.
