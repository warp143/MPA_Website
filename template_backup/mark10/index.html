Word From The President

Dr. Daniele Gambero, MPA President

Welcome to you, the visitor of the Malaysia PropTech Association website. Let me give you a very short introduction of what our association is and what is offering to all our members, associates, visitors, and more.

What we do is simple, we have been pulling together as many as possible of the Malaysian tech start-ups in property and construction with the aim of becoming a loud voice towards the implementation of digital transformation in the built environment.

Our Goals:

  • Making Malaysia Proptech Association The Recognised Industry Association in Malaysia and beyond
  • Exposing all our members to international VCs and funding opportunities
  • Making MPA a global reference association of emerging markets
  • Seeing at least one of the Malaysian Proptech/ConTech startups, member of MPA, to raise to unicorn status

MPA's Pillars

MPA is committed to building a strong community on its pillars with integrity, inclusivity, and equality of all PropTech Members.

Promote

MPA will promote all members, stakeholders, and strategic partners.

Inspire

MPA is here to inspire its members, students, fresh grads, and the property industry at large.

Events

MPA members organize and participate in global events.

Partnerships

MPA actively seeks for partnerships in Malaysia & abroad.

Employment

MPA believes in education and deployment within the PropTech industry

Innovation

MPA helps to drive the PropTech Industry towards higher levels of innovation and technology.

Why Join MPA?

Together We Stand Strong - MPA is committed to the integrity, inclusivity, equality, and growth of all PropTech Members

Strong Outreach Through Events

Participate in our local and internationally promoted events. MPA believes in a strong outreach within the local tech world, but also into the local property and construction industry. Events both virtual and physical provide the ultimate opportunity to obtain knowledge and meet like-minded people.

Partnerships Form Our Foundation

Respecting each other's values drives innovation. We fully understand that taking on one of the largest industries in the country and feeding them with the latest technology and innovations, is a mammoth task. Only through strategic partnerships, we will succeed to reach even the furthest outcrops of the industry.

Funding Moves Businesses To Greater Heights

In a relatively young industry start-ups and scale-ups flourish and thrive. Asia's strong funding ecosystem is gaining even more strength with international players taking a peek at the Asian PropTech landscape continuously. Funding for the industry, both local and overseas are important drivers for technology and homegrown innovations.
