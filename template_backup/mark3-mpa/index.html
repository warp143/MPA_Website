About MPA

Empowering Malaysia's proptech ecosystem through collaboration and innovation

Our Mission

The Malaysia Proptech Association (MPA) is the leading industry body dedicated to advancing property technology in Malaysia. We bring together startups, established companies, investors, and government agencies to foster innovation and digital transformation in the real estate sector.
