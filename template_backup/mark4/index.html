Our Mission

MPA is committed to building a strong community on its pillars with integrity, inclusivity, and equality of all PropTech Members. We pull together Malaysian tech start-ups in property and construction with the aim of becoming a loud voice towards the implementation of digital transformation in the built environment.

Promote

MPA will promote all members, stakeholders, and strategic partners to create maximum visibility and opportunities.

Inspire

MPA is here to inspire its members, students, fresh grads, and the property industry at large with innovative solutions.

Events

MPA members organize and participate in global events to showcase Malaysian PropTech innovation on the world stage.

Partnerships

MPA actively seeks for partnerships in Malaysia & abroad to strengthen the PropTech ecosystem.

Employment

MPA believes in education and deployment within the PropTech industry to build talent and expertise.

Innovation

MPA helps to drive the PropTech Industry towards higher levels of innovation and technology adoption.
