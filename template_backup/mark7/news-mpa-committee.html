MPA New Committee Members 2021-2023

The Malaysian PropTech Association voted in a new and extended committee to lead the organization for the 2021-2023 tenure, bringing fresh perspectives and expertise to drive innovation and growth in the PropTech ecosystem.

New Leadership for a New Era

The Malaysia PropTech Association (MPA) has announced the election of its new committee members for the 2021-2023 tenure. This election marks a significant milestone in the organization's growth and represents a strategic shift towards greater innovation, collaboration, and industry leadership in the PropTech space.

Election Process and Results

The election process involved active participation from MPA members, with candidates nominated from various segments of the PropTech ecosystem including startups, established companies, academic institutions, and government agencies. The final committee composition reflects the diverse nature of the PropTech industry and ensures representation from all key stakeholders.

New Committee Structure

The new committee has been expanded to include additional positions and specialized roles to better serve the growing needs of the PropTech community:

Executive Committee

  • President: Dr. Daniele Gambero - Leading the digital transformation with 15+ years of experience
  • Deputy President: Jason Ding - Driving innovation and strategic partnerships
  • Secretary: Wong Keh Wei - Managing operations and member communications
  • Treasurer: Liz Ang Gaik See - Overseeing financial management and budgeting

Vice Presidents

  • VP Membership: Angela Kew Chui Teen - Expanding member base and engagement
  • VP Events: Naga R Krishnan - Organizing industry events and conferences
  • VP Technology: New position focused on technology innovation and standards
  • VP International Relations: New position for global partnerships and collaboration

Key Objectives for 2021-2023

The new committee has outlined several key objectives to guide MPA's activities over the next two years:

1. Membership Growth and Engagement

The committee aims to significantly expand MPA's membership base while improving engagement and value delivery to existing members. This includes:

  • Targeting 200+ active members by 2023
  • Implementing enhanced member benefits and services
  • Creating specialized membership categories for different stakeholder groups
  • Developing mentorship and networking programs

2. Industry Innovation and Standards

MPA will focus on driving innovation and establishing industry standards:

  • Establishing PropTech innovation labs and incubators
  • Developing industry best practices and standards
  • Creating technology adoption frameworks
  • Facilitating research and development partnerships

3. Education and Capacity Building

The committee will prioritize education and skill development:

  • Launching comprehensive PropTech education programs
  • Organizing workshops and training sessions
  • Developing certification programs for PropTech professionals
  • Creating resources for students and fresh graduates

4. International Collaboration

Expanding MPA's global reach and partnerships:

  • Establishing partnerships with international PropTech associations
  • Participating in global PropTech events and conferences
  • Facilitating cross-border investment and collaboration opportunities
  • Promoting Malaysian PropTech companies internationally

Committee Member Profiles

Dr. Daniele Gambero - President

Dr. Gambero brings over 15 years of experience in digital transformation and PropTech innovation. His leadership will focus on establishing MPA as a leading voice in the Asia Pacific PropTech ecosystem and driving strategic initiatives that benefit all members.

Jason Ding - Deputy President

Jason Ding's expertise in innovation and strategic partnerships will be instrumental in expanding MPA's network and creating new opportunities for collaboration between traditional real estate companies and PropTech innovators.

Wong Keh Wei - Secretary

Wong Keh Wei will oversee MPA's day-to-day operations and ensure effective communication with members and stakeholders. His role is crucial in maintaining transparency and accountability within the organization.

Liz Ang Gaik See - Treasurer

Liz Ang will manage MPA's financial affairs and ensure sustainable growth through effective financial planning and resource allocation.

Strategic Initiatives

The new committee has identified several strategic initiatives to be launched in the coming months:

PropTech Innovation Hub

MPA will establish a physical innovation hub to serve as a center for PropTech development, collaboration, and networking. The hub will provide co-working spaces, meeting facilities, and technology infrastructure for startups and established companies.

Industry Research and Reports

The association will conduct regular research on PropTech trends, market analysis, and industry insights to provide valuable information to members and stakeholders.

Policy Advocacy

MPA will actively engage with government agencies and regulatory bodies to advocate for policies that support PropTech innovation and growth.

Member Benefits and Services

The new committee has committed to enhancing member benefits and services:

  • Exclusive Events: Members-only conferences, workshops, and networking events
  • Resource Access: Access to research reports, market insights, and industry data
  • Networking Opportunities: Regular networking events and matchmaking services
  • Professional Development: Training programs and certification opportunities
  • Business Support: Mentorship programs and business development assistance

Industry Impact

The new committee's leadership is expected to have a significant impact on the Malaysian PropTech industry:

Increased Innovation

The focus on innovation and technology standards will drive the development of new PropTech solutions and improve existing ones.

Enhanced Collaboration

The expanded committee structure will facilitate better collaboration between different segments of the PropTech ecosystem.

Improved Market Position

MPA's enhanced international presence will help position Malaysia as a leading PropTech hub in the Asia Pacific region.

Future Outlook

The new committee's tenure coincides with a period of significant growth and transformation in the PropTech industry. The committee is well-positioned to capitalize on these opportunities and drive MPA's continued success.

Expected Achievements

By 2023, the committee aims to achieve:

  • 200+ active members representing diverse stakeholder groups
  • Established PropTech innovation hub and incubator programs
  • Strong international partnerships and global presence
  • Comprehensive education and certification programs
  • Significant policy influence and industry recognition

Conclusion

The election of the new MPA committee for 2021-2023 represents an important milestone in the organization's development. The expanded committee structure, diverse expertise, and strategic focus on innovation and collaboration position MPA for continued growth and success.

The Malaysia PropTech Association looks forward to working with all stakeholders to advance the PropTech industry and create value for members and the broader real estate ecosystem.
